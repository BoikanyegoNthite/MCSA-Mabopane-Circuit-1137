Our Mission

As the Methodist Church of Southern Africa, our mission is to spread the teachings of Jesus Christ, promote spiritual growth, and serve our communities with love and compassion. Our core values include:

Faith: We are committed to living out our faith in Jesus Christ through worship, prayer, and discipleship. Love: We strive to love one another as Christ loved us, showing compassion, kindness, and generosity to all people.

Service: We are called to serve others, especially the marginalized and vulnerable, through acts of mercy, justice, and reconciliation.

Evangelism: We are dedicated to sharing the good news of salvation with others and inviting them to join us in following Christ.

Our Ministries

These ministries work together to create a holistic approach to fulfilling the Methodist Church of Southern Africa's mission, reaching people at various stages of life and addressing both their spiritual and practical needs. Through these efforts, the church seeks to make a positive impact in Southern Africa and beyond, transforming lives and communities with the love and grace of God

Worship and Prayer: Providing opportunities for congregants to come together in worship, prayer, and spiritual reflection, whether through regular Sunday services, midweek prayer meetings, or special events such as retreats and conferences.

Discipleship and Education: Offering programs and resources to help members grow in their faith and understanding of Scripture, including Bible studies, discipleship courses, Sunday school classes, and theological training for ministers and lay leaders. Outreach and Evangelism: Engaging in evangelistic efforts to share the gospel message with those who have not yet heard it, through activities such as community outreach events, evangelistic campaigns, and mission trips to areas in need
